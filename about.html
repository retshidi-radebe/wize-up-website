About Wize Up

Welcome to Wize Up

Wize Up is dedicated to promoting education, mathematics, science, and cultural cooperation in South Africa. With over 6 years of experience, we integrate research, monitoring, and evaluation at every stage to ensure impactful programming.

Sustainability lies at the core of our programme designs adopting a social entrepreneurship mindset and approach to the foundation. We strive for our programmes to strengthen the development of people, communities, companies and governments to realise the right to education and quality of life Using a range of research methods tailored to the context, and drawing on our extensive experience and knowledge of the sector, we examine a range of possibilities on behalf of our clients

Mission

Our Mission

is to contribute to local economic development by implementing education and development programs for children and youth with measurable social impact and maximum returns for all stakeholders.

Diagnosis

The reality is that a child's experience of education in South Africa still depends on where they are born, how wealthy they are, and the colour of their skin. Students from poor communities experience problems with high fees, lack of books, deteriorated facilities, lack of teachers, and lack of high-quality instruction. They face many socio-economic challenges linked to poverty such as hunger, violence, high rates of crime, high drop-out rates, high rates of teenage pregnancy, and increased vulnerability to HIV.

These factors nourish the poverty cycle, resulting in low levels of skills for youth in poor communities and increasing rates of unemployment. Local businesses thus struggle to source local talent, which results in them recruiting their labour force from areas outside their host communities. This creates social unrest and increases the cost of labour. Investing in education will result in more impactful development of these communities and contribute towards breaking the poverty cycle for future generations.

Solution

Our Solution

Our programmes deliver exceptional matric performance for township and rural learners through an eco system that helps them with the transition from basic to higher education and a support system to increase probability of s ucc e s s in institutions of higher learning.

Performance Review

The Wize Up Maths & Science program is currently implemented in Carolina Circuit in Mpumalanga and Diepkloof Zone 3 in Soweto, Gauteng. The program started in 2021, enrolling approximately 120 Grade 11 learners from three schools (Zinikeleni Secondary, Violet Jiyane & Carolina Combined). The learners progressed to Grade 12 and remained part of the program, which supported them throughout their learning journey.

The following illustrates the programme contribution to learner performance after the first year. The stats show the learner performance at the end of Grade 10 in 2020 compared to the end of Grade 11 in 2021.

"Grade 11 is a critical year for learners because the end-of-year results are what learners use for their initial application into University. It is amazing to see how much impact the program has had on our schools in just one year. We have never seen such results in this circuit. This is a significant improvement, and at this rate, I believe we will see even better performance when the learners finish Grade 12."

- Dr Mngomezulu, Director DBE Carolina Circuit.
